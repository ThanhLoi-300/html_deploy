Bomber Game

Imagine you and your three friends are getting ready for an exciting and challenging adventure. You have found an interesting game called "BOOMING GAME EMPIRE" - a dramatic, exciting and dynamic boom game, specially designed for two players. In "Bomber Game", each player takes on the role of an adventurous character ready to take on dangerous challenges. The game takes place in a large maze where the player must find a way to make their way and defeat the enemies to progress to the next level.
